Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a team?

Tap the "+" button on the Teams screen to create a new team.

How do I add players?

Go to the Players tab and tap the "+" button to add a new player.

How do I track game statistics?

Navigate to a game and use the "Quick Stats Entry" button to quickly record statistics during the game.

Privacy

Statshots stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit any personal information.
